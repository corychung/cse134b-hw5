FAQ

How long have you been 3D printing?

Since 2017! My parents bought me a 3D printer kit called the "Anet A8" made of acrylic and a lot of loose wires (also famous for fire hazards but luckily not mine). Ever since then, I've worked with various other brands, tinkering and upgrading, and currently use a Bambu A1 mini.

Why choose UI/UX with such a maker background?

I've always been a tinkerer but for some reason I never thought of my background there as a potential career choice. I like keeping it separate as it gives me the freedom to explore my own fields rather than pressure to adhere to some job criteria. Plus when it comes to UI/UX I like solving problems, so working towards a set goal makes things much easier.

What software tools do you use?

While it tends to vary as I love trying out new things, here are my current standards:

  • Figma - UI/UX Design
  • Fusion - 3D Modeling
  • Orca Slicer, Bambu Studio - 3D Printer Slicing
  • VS Code - Web Development

I've also been tinkering with new 2D design programs like Affinity Designer and video editing programs like DaVinci Resolve!
